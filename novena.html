The Litany of Loreto, Act of Contrition

Every Day

Prayer

Most powerful and merciful Lord Jesus, lover of souls, most admirable in Thy saints; listen to the sighs and pleas of the souls in Purgatory who are desirous of Thy mercy and to the prayers of those who are making this novena for them, so that they may obtain Thy mercy and deign to rescue them from that prison of their great suffering. I beseech Thee to relieve first those who are suffering there through my fault, my close relatives, the abandoned, and those who are about to be released from Purgatory. I beseech Thee also, to free above all, those who injured me most and deliver from their sins those who are in the bondage of mortal sin and bring them to a sincere repentance of their sins, and become worthy of Heaven. Amen.

Prayer for Each Day

First Day

Pray three Hail Holy Queen's to the Blessed Virgin, then say the prayer for this day.

My Lord, receive my supplications, prayers, mortifications and sighs in suffrage for the blessed souls for whom I make this novena; and I pray that by the motherly love bestowed on Thee by the Blessed Virgin, Thou mayest grant tht they may arise and be happy in the realm if the living; bestow upon me Thy grace by those sorrows endured by Thy most Holy Mother, when She followed Thee on the Street of Sorrow up to Mt. Calvary, and grant what I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Second Day

Pray five Our Father's and Hail Mary's in honor of the five Wounds of Jesus and the great sorrows of Mary, then say the prayer for this day.

Most merciful Lord, I beseech Thee, by that pain with which Thy Holy Mother saw Thee suffer and agonize on the Sacred Tree of the Cross, that the holy souls be freed from those pains in Purgatory; especially those souls for whom I am praying in this novena, applying the indulgence granted by the Supreme Pontiffs for their relief to all Religious, Fraternities and Congregations. Bring those who are submerged in their sins to a true knowledge of their guilt and grant what I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Third Day

Pray three Our Father's, Hail Mary's, and Glory's in honor of Saint Joseph, then say the prayer for this day.

Almighty God and Lord, to whom nobody asks without hope of receiving, by the intercession of that glorious Patriarch Saint Joseph, and by the patronage of Thy beloved Mother, particularly by that sorrow She experienced when She saw Thee expire on the Cross, enable the suffering souls in Purgatory to be able to leave that place. Deign to return to Thy grace those who are without it because of sin, and grant me what I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Fourth Day

Pray four Our Father's, Hail Mary's, and Glory's in honor of St. Joachim and St. Anne, then say the prayer for this day.

O great God, through Whose mercy the saints rest in glory, I beg Thee to set free those blessed souls for whom I am specially praying, through the interciessions of St. Joachim and St. Anne. Hear me Lord, by that bitter pain of Thy afflicted Mother when She heard those outrageous blasphemies and offensive words with which Thy enemies were insulting Thy Divine Innocence. I beg Thee, Lord, to have mercy on those who follow the path of perdition by making them return to Thy grace; and please grant me what I ask in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Fifth Day

Pray four Our Father's and Hail Mary's in honor of St. Michael, the Archangel, then say the prayer for this day.

Sovereign Lord, in Whom it is prosper to be merciful, through the intercession of the Archangel St. Michael and by the sorrow of Thy Blessed Mother suffered when that soldier cruelly pierced Thy side with a lance, have pity on those souls for whom I am offering this novena and bring them to Thy eternal rest. Have merct on those who are in the state of mortal sin and grant me what I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Sixth Day

Pray five Our Father's, Hail Mary's, and Glory's in reverence of the glorious St. Lawrence, special advocate of the souls, then say the prayer for this day.

Sweetest Jesus, incline Thine ears to my petitions for Thy merct on the blessed souls in Purgatory, especially on those for whom I am making this novena, through the torments suffered by the glorious St. Lawrence in the fire of his martyrdom. I am also begging Thy mercy for the blessed souls in Purgatory by the pains suffered by Thy loving Mother when She saw Thee being taken down from the Cross and when She received in Her Sacred Hands the Crown and Nails bathed in Thy Precious Blood. Have pity on those who are not in Thy grace by taking them out of their unhappy state and grant me what I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Seventh Day

Pray nine Our Father's and Hail Mary's in honor of the nine choirs of Angels, then say the prayer for this day.

Merciful Lord, through the intercession of the Angels, especially of those who are the guardians of those afflicted souls for whom I make this novena; and by that sorrow of Thy Blessed Mother when, after having kissed and adored Thy Crown and Nails, She received Thee in Her virginal and maternal arms and gazed on Thy mangled and lacerated Body, I humbly beeech Thee to set them free and bring them to the happiness of Heaven. Lead those who are in mortal sin to a true knowledge of their guilt and grant me the grace I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Eighth Day

Pray five Our Father's and Hail Mary's in honor of the Seraphic Father St. Francis abd St. Nicolas of Tolentino, special patrons of the Blessed souls, then say the prayer for this day.

My Sweet and beloved Jesus, through the intercession of Thy beloved seraphim, St. Francis, and of the glorious and charitable St. Nicolas of Tolentino, I beg of Thee to shower the immense treasures of Thy clemency on those blessed souls whose torments so touched the charity of these two Servants of Thine. But I beg this grace especially through that bleeding Heart of Thy Blessed Mother when She wrapped and clothed Thy Sacred Body for burial and accompanied It to the Sepulchre. Reduce those who are in sin to a true repentance of them and grant me what I ask in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Ninth Day

Pray five time the Apostles' Creed in honor of the Passion of Our Lord Jesus Christ, then say the prayer for this day.

Creator and Redeemer of all mankind, through the infinite merits of Thy Life, Passion, and Death, I beg of Thee to bestow on all souls, particularly those for hom I am making this novena, the pardon and indulgences they always desired. Through the intercession and merits of Thy afflicted Mother, especially by that sorrow of sorrows with which She left Thee buried in the tomb and returned home without the light of Her eyes and the life of Her heart, which is Thou, my Jesus, have mercy on the blessed souls so that they may enjoy Thee eternally in Heaven. Turn Thine eyes of mercy, too, on those who are in the miserable state of slavery of the devil, so that they may be restored to Thy grace, and grant me what I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Decade of the Passion

Lord, open our lips and inflame our hearts and cleanse them of useless and evil thoughts. Enlighten our minds, that we may seriously meditate on Thy sufferings and death and the pains endured by Thy most glorious Mother; and deign to hear and receive us before Thy great Majesty, Thou who livet and reignest forever. Amen.


On the beads of the rosary for the Our Father, say the following:-


My most merciful Jesus, look down with eyes of pity on the faithful souls for whom Thou suffered and died on the Cross.


On the beads for the Hail Mary, say the following:-


L: My Jesus, through Thy bloody sweat in the Garden,

R: Have mercy on the souls in Purgatory.


L: My Jesus, through the blow Thou received on Thy Sacred Face,

R: Have mercy on the souls in Purgatory.


L: My Jesus, through the cruel scourging Thou endured,

R: Have mercy on the souls in Purgatory.


L: My Jesus, through the crown of thorns that pierced Thy Head,

R: Have mercy on the souls in Purgatory.


L: My Jesus, through Thy carrying of the Cross on the path of bitterness,

R: Have mercy on the souls in Purgatory.


L: My Jesus, through Thy face covered with blood which Thou allowed to be imprinted on the veil of Veronica,

R: Have mercy on the souls in Purgatory.


L: My Jesus, through Thy bloody garments which were cruelly removed from Thy wounded Body,

R: Have mercy on the souls in Purgatory.


L: My Jesus, through Thy Holy Body nailed on the Cross,

R: Have mercy on the souls in Purgatory.


L: My Jesus, through Thy Hands and Feet pierced with cruel nails,

R: Have mercy on the souls in Purgatory.


L: My Jesus, through Thy Sacred Side pierced with a lance and from which flowed blood and water.

R: Have mercy on the souls in Purgatory.

OFFERING: (an excerpt from a prayer of St. Augustine)

Most sweet Jesus, Who in Thy desire to redeem the world, deigned to be born, circumcised, mocked by the Jews, to fall into the hands of Thy enemies through the treacherous kiss of Judas, to be bound and brought to the place of sacrafice like a meek Lamb, brought before Caiphas, Pilate, and Herod, soat on, accused and condemned with false witnesses, to be slapped and have Thy Sacred Body covered with wounds from the cruel scourging, to be covered with thorns, Thy Face covered with a purple cloth in mockery, stripped of Thy garments, nailed and elevated on the Cross between two thieves as if Thou wert one of them, offered gail and vinegar to drink, and to be pierced in Thy Side with a lance, deliver, O Lord, the souls in Purgatory from their great sufferings through Thy most bitter Passion; bring them to Thy Glory and deliver us from the pains of hell, through the merits of Thy Holy Passion and Death on the Cross, so that we may be worthy to enter that Kingdom where Thou brought the good thief, who was crucified with Thee, who liveth and reigneth with that Father and the Holy Ghost forever. Amen.
