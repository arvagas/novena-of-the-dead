The Apostles' Creed

I believe in God, the Father almighty, Creator of heaven and earth, and in Jesus Christ, his only Son, our Lord, who was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary, suffered under Pontius Pilate, was crucified, died and was buried; he descended into hell; on the third day he rose again from the dead; he ascended into heaven, and is seated at the right hand of God the Father almighty; from there he will come to judge the living and the dead. I believe in the Holy Spirit, the holy catholic Church, the communion of saints, the forgiveness of sins, the resurrection of the body, and life everlasting. Amen.

Act of Contrition

O my God, I am heartily sorry for having offended you. I detest all my sins because of your just punishments. But most of all because they offend you, my God, who are all good and deserving of all my love. I firmly resolve, with the help of your grace to sin no more and to avoid the near occasions of sin. Amen.

Prayer

Most powerful and merciful Lord Jesus, lover of souls, most admirable in Thy saints; listen to the sighs and pleas of the souls in Purgatory who are desirous of Thy mercy and to the prayers of those who are making this novena for them, so that they may obtain Thy mercy and deign to rescue them from that prison of their great suffering. I beseech Thee to relieve first those who are suffering there through my fault, my close relatives, the abandoned, and those who are about to be released from Purgatory. I beseech Thee also, to free above all, those who injured me most and deliver from their sins those who are in the bondage of mortal sin and bring them to a sincere repentance of their sins, and become worthy of Heaven. Amen.

Prayer for Each Day

First Day


Pray three Hail Holy Queen's to the Blessed Virgin, then say the prayer for this day.

My Lord, receive my supplications, prayers, mortifications and sighs in suffrage for the blessed souls for whom I make this novena; and I pray that by the motherly love bestowed on Thee by the Blessed Virgin, Thou mayest grant tht they may arise and be happy in the realm if the living; bestow upon me Thy grace by those sorrows endured by Thy most Holy Mother, when She followed Thee on the Street of Sorrow up to Mt. Calvary, and grant what I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Second Day


Pray five Our Father's and Hail Mary's in honor of the five Wounds of Jesus and the great sorrows of Mary, then say the prayer for this day.

Most merciful Lord, I beseech Thee, by that pain with which Thy Holy Mother saw Thee suffer and agonize on the Sacred Tree of the Cross, that the holy souls be freed from those pains in Purgatory; especially those souls for whom I am praying in this novena, applying the indulgence granted by the Supreme Pontiffs for their relief to all Religious, Fraternities and Congregations. Bring those who are submerged in their sins to a true knowledge of their guilt and grant what I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Third Day


Pray three Our Father's, Hail Mary's, and Glory's in honor of Saint Joseph, then say the prayer for this day.

Almighty God and Lord, to whom nobody asks without hope of receiving, by the intercession of that glorious Patriarch Saint Joseph, and by the patronage of Thy beloved Mother, particularly by that sorrow She experienced when She saw Thee expire on the Cross, enable the suffering souls in Purgatory to be able to leave that place. Deign to return to Thy grace those who are without it because of sin, and grant me what I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Fourth Day


Pray four Our Father's, Hail Mary's, and Glory's in honor of St. Joachim and St. Anne, then say the prayer for this day.

O great God, through Whose mercy the saints rest in glory, I beg Thee to set free those blessed souls for whom I am specially praying, through the intercessions of St. Joachim and St. Anne. Hear me Lord, by that bitter pain of Thy afflicted Mother when She heard those outrageous blasphemies and offensive words with which Thy enemies were insulting Thy Divine Innocence. I beg Thee, Lord, to have mercy on those who follow the path of perdition by making them return to Thy grace; and please grant me what I ask in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Fifth Day


Pray four Our Father's and Hail Mary's in honor of St. Michael, the Archangel, then say the prayer for this day.

Sovereign Lord, in Whom it is proper to be merciful, through the intercession of the Archangel St. Michael and by the sorrow of Thy Blessed Mother suffered when that soldier cruelly pierced Thy side with a lance, have pity on those souls for whom I am offering this novena and bring them to Thy eternal rest. Have mercy on those who are in the state of mortal sin and grant me what I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Sixth Day


Pray five Our Father's, Hail Mary's, and Glory's in reverence of the glorious St. Lawrence, special advocate of the souls, then say the prayer for this day.

Sweetest Jesus, incline Thine ears to my petitions for Thy mercy on the blessed souls in Purgatory, especially on those for whom I am making this novena, through the torments suffered by the glorious St. Lawrence in the fire of his martyrdom. I am also begging Thy mercy for the blessed souls in Purgatory by the pains suffered by Thy loving Mother when She saw Thee being taken down from the Cross and when She received in Her Sacred Hands the Crown and Nails bathed in Thy Precious Blood. Have pity on those who are not in Thy grace by taking them out of their unhappy state and grant me what I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Seventh Day


Pray nine Our Father's and Hail Mary's in honor of the nine choirs of Angels, then say the prayer for this day.

Merciful Lord, through the intercession of the Angels, especially of those who are the guardians of those afflicted souls for whom I make this novena; and by that sorrow of Thy Blessed Mother when, after having kissed and adored Thy Crown and Nails, She received Thee in Her virginal and maternal arms and gazed on Thy mangled and lacerated Body, I humbly beeech Thee to set them free and bring them to the happiness of Heaven. Lead those who are in mortal sin to a true knowledge of their guilt and grant me the grace I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Eighth Day


Pray five Our Father's and Hail Mary's in honor of the Seraphic Father St. Francis and St. Nicolas of Tolentino, special patrons of the Blessed souls, then say the prayer for this day.

My Sweet and beloved Jesus, through the intercession of Thy beloved seraphim, St. Francis, and of the glorious and charitable St. Nicolas of Tolentino, I beg of Thee to shower the immense treasures of Thy clemency on those blessed souls whose torments so touched the charity of these two Servants of Thine. But I beg this grace especially through that bleeding Heart of Thy Blessed Mother when She wrapped and clothed Thy Sacred Body for burial and accompanied It to the Sepulchre. Reduce those who are in sin to a true repentance of them and grant me what I ask in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Ninth Day


Pray five time the Apostles' Creed in honor of the Passion of Our Lord Jesus Christ, then say the prayer for this day.

Creator and Redeemer of all mankind, through the infinite merits of Thy Life, Passion, and Death, I beg of Thee to bestow on all souls, particularly those for whom I am making this novena, the pardon and indulgences they always desired. Through the intercession and merits of Thy afflicted Mother, especially by that sorrow of sorrows with which She left Thee buried in the tomb and returned home without the light of Her eyes and the life of Her heart, which is Thou, my Jesus, have mercy on the blessed souls so that they may enjoy Thee eternally in Heaven. Turn Thine eyes of mercy, too, on those who are in the miserable state of slavery of the devil, so that they may be restored to Thy grace, and grant me what I ask of Thee in this novena for Thy greater honor and glory. Amen.

Decade of the Passion

Lord, open our lips and inflame our hearts and cleanse them of useless and evil thoughts. Enlighten our minds, that we may seriously meditate on Thy sufferings and death and the pains endured by Thy most glorious Mother; and deign to hear and receive us before Thy great Majesty, Thou who liveth and reignest forever. Amen.

Offering

{ Excerpt from a prayer of St. Augustine }


Most sweet Jesus, Who in Thy desire to redeem the world, deigned to be born, circumcised, mocked by the Jews, to fall into the hands of Thy enemies through the treacherous kiss of Judas, to be bound and brought to the place of sacrifice like a meek Lamb, brought before Caiphas, Pilate, and Herod, spat on, accused and condemned with false witnesses, to be slapped and have Thy Sacred Body covered with wounds from the cruel scourging, to be crowned with thorns, Thy Face covered with a purple cloth in mockery, stripped of Thy garments, nailed and elevated on the Cross between two thieves as if Thou wert one of them, offered gall and vinegar to drink, and to be pierced in Thy Side with a lance, deliver, O Lord, the souls in Purgatory from their great sufferings through Thy most bitter Passion; bring them to Thy Glory and deliver us from the pains of hell, through the merits of Thy Holy Passion and Death on the Cross, so that we may be worthy to enter that Kingdom where Thou brought the good thief, who was crucified with Thee, who liveth and reigneth with that Father and the Holy Ghost forever. Amen.

Words of Pope Saint Gregory

First Day

O Jesus Christ, my Lord, Who to redeem me was crowned with thorns and was crucified, I adore and beseech Thee that Thy Cross be my defense against my evil enemies.


Our Father and Hail Mary.

Second Day

O Jesus Christ, my Lord, Who to redeem me, endured so much sufferings and drank gall and vinegar, I adore and beseech Thee that Thy sufferings be the cure of my soul.


Our Father and Hail Mary.

Third Day

O Jesus Christ, my lord, by the bitterness Thou suffered on the Cross because of my sins, particularly when Thy Soul parted from Thy Body, I beseech Thee to have mercy on my soul on the day of its departure from this world.


Our Father and Hail Mary.

Fourth Day

O Jesus Christ, my Lord, Who to redeem me, Thy Sacred Body was annointed and buried, I adore and beseech Thee that Thy death be my life.


Our Father and Hail Mary.

Fifth Day

O Jesus Christ, my Lord, Who descended to the limbo of the Holy Fathers and freed those who were imprisoned there, I adore and beseech Thee not to permit my soul to be imprisoned in hell.


Our Father and Hail Mary.

Sixth Day

O Jesus Christ, my Lord, Who with great power resurrected Thyself and ascended to Heaven, where Thou sitteth at the right of God the Father, I pray Thee to have mercy on us.


Our Father and Hail Mary.

Seventh Day

O Jesus Christ, my Lord, the Good Shepherd, defend the just, enlighten the sinners, have mercy on the faithful departed, and on me, great sinner.


Our Father and Hail Mary.

Eighth Day

O Jesus Christ, my Lord, Who will come to judge us, to bring the just to Thy glory and the sinners to eternal punishment, I adore and beseech Thee that Thy Passion may free me from all pains and bring me to eternal life.


Our Father and Hail Mary.

Ninth Day

O most loving Father, I offer Thee the innocent death of Thy Son and the loves of His Divine Heart for the pains that I, a poor sinner, above all other sinners, deserve; I also offer this Passion and cordial love for all my relatives and friends. I beseech Thee to have mercy on us.


Our Father and Hail Mary.

Every Day

O Jesus Christ, my Lord, I humbly beseech Thee to grant me the pardon and graces that are accorded by St. Gregory to those who, through these holy prayers revere the mysteries of Thy great Passion, Who liveth and reigneth for ever and ever.


Our Father and Hail Mary.

Final Prayer

O Lord God, Who left us the marks of Thy Passion on the Holy Sheet, in which Thy Sacred Body was wrapped when It was taken down from the Cross by Joseph, grant us, Lord, that by Thy death and burial, we may be brought to the glory of the Resurrection, where Thou liveth and reigneth with the Father and the Holy Ghost, God for ever and ever. Amen.
